Biography

My name is Kevin, I am a full stack software engineer with 5 years of experience that loves to work with new and innovative technologies. I am excited to transform incredible ideas into reliable, scalable and easy to use software to help businesses to make a dent in the universe.

I have experience in developing solutions from zero to hero with HTML5, CSS，JavaScript，Angular 2+，ReactJS，Java, SpringBoot, Node.js, Express, Hibernate， MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, SQL Server, Git and AWS.

I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2019. I worked as full stack web developer in SECURE AND INTELLIGENT THINGS LAB. for around two years. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2016.

Currently, I am working at BNY Mellon as a full stack software engineer (consultant) for around 1 year for a project called NEXEN, which is using Angular 9, Typescript, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Webpack, grunt, Java, SpringBoot, and MySQL.
